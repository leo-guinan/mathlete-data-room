Machine Lagging

Consider the average ML pipeline:
Data collected (T-0)
Model trained (T-30 days)
Deployed (T-45 days)
Making "real-time" decisions (T+∞)

This isn't machine learning—it's machine lagging.

Your AI is making decisions with information that's already obsolete.
