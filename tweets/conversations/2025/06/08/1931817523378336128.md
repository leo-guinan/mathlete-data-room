Time Violence Measurement
You can quantify temporal gaps in your systems:
TV(Π,t) = ∫[H(τ) - S(τ)]² dτ
Where H(τ) = human temporal position, S(τ) = system temporal position

Most systems have massive, unmeasured time violence.
