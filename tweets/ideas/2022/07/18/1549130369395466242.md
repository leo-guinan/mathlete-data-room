Don't ask "what do you do?" at parties.

IMO also avoid "what's your story?" and "so how do you know [host]?"

Better options:
-which old god is your patron?
-what's your favorite form of ritual sacrifice?
-have you tried the goblet of "wine" on the altar in the corner?
