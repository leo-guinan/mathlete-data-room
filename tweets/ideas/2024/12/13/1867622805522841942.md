Things become a lot easier to measure and predict when we stop trying to measure and predict them.

Don't measure, collect.

Don't predict, analyze.

Predictions should be made at least one layer beyond the desired metric. 

Don't just predict what. Predict why. https://t.co/gODV0905xl
