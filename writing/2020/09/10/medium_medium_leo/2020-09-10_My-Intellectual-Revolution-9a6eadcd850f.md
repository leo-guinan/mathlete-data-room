And what I Learned from it

<section class="e-content" data-field="body">
<section class="section section--body section--first section--last" name="68a8"><div class="section-divider"><hr class="section-divider"/></div><div class="section-content"><div class="section-inner sectionLayout--insetColumn"><h3 class="graf graf--h3 graf--leading graf--title" id="8b50" name="8b50">My Intellectual Revolution</h3><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--h3" id="c165" name="c165">And what I Learned from it</p><figure class="graf graf--figure graf-after--p" id="ab37" name="ab37"><img alt="Stenciled lettering reading “Think Before You Speak. Read Before You Think”" class="graf-image" data-height="5334" data-image-id="0*M5K_GIUeyon5TfLN" data-is-featured="true" data-unsplash-photo-id="gcw_WWu_uBQ" data-width="3556" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*M5K_GIUeyon5TfLN"/><figcaption class="imageCaption">Photo by <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com/@kylejglenn?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com/@kylejglenn?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-creator noopener" target="_blank">Kyle Glenn</a> on <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-source noopener" target="_blank">Unsplash</a></figcaption></figure><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--figure" id="b795" name="b795">I was hesitant to use those words as a title, but since I did so, let’s define them.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="12cb" name="12cb">Intellectual — referring to the way I think about the world</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="b75f" name="b75f">Revolution — a great deal of change in a relatively short period of time.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="0d0f" name="0d0f">I have been working on shaping the way I think for years. I have always felt that there was some way I could optimize my brain and let me make leaps that no one else could.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="c941" name="c941">And the leaps never came. I got better at bits and pieces of my life and career. But I never made a leap.</p><figure class="graf graf--figure graf-after--p" id="3316" name="3316"><img alt="View overlooking cliff" class="graf-image" data-height="3888" data-image-id="0*7AW3SGg10RdxKDUg" data-unsplash-photo-id="-k480xJCWvM" data-width="2592" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*7AW3SGg10RdxKDUg"/><figcaption class="imageCaption">Photo by <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com/@eddybllrd?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com/@eddybllrd?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-creator noopener" target="_blank">Eddy Billard</a> on <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-source noopener" target="_blank">Unsplash</a></figcaption></figure><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--figure" id="61dd" name="61dd">Until I did.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="d82d" name="d82d">It took viewing problems from a different angle. I have been working on understanding the world through my view of it. What I needed to do was to understand the world through everyone’s view of it. And the perspective I gained was invaluable.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="c7e8" name="c7e8">We are taught in life to be scientists. We pose a question. We form a hypothesis.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="4796" name="4796">The hypothesis is the problem. Usually we define is as an <em class="markup--em markup--p-em">educated</em> guess.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="1f67" name="1f67">Educated is the problem. What does educated mean? It means we <em class="markup--em markup--p-em">think</em> we know something. What if we don’t make an educated guess? What if we just make a guess? What if we don’t even bother with that?</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="8bb4" name="8bb4">What if we just do experiments in the real world and see what happens?</p><figure class="graf graf--figure graf-after--p" id="809a" name="809a"><img alt="lights on wires coming out of central pole" class="graf-image" data-height="4032" data-image-id="0*PwmVLETgFZ7Dm7uk" data-unsplash-photo-id="RBnP_OdmTeE" data-width="3024" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*PwmVLETgFZ7Dm7uk"/><figcaption class="imageCaption">Photo by <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com/@bfigas?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com/@bfigas?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-creator noopener" target="_blank">Bruno Figueiredo</a> on <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-source noopener" target="_blank">Unsplash</a></figcaption></figure><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--figure" id="493b" name="493b">We are connected now in a way we never were before. I think we need to shift from being specialized to being more general, more curious. We can figure out the education part after the observation. That got shifted somewhere. So right now, we are working on educating and then observing the world. That is backward from where it needs to be, in my opinion.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="2d29" name="2d29">So I am shifting my perspective. I am assuming I know nothing about anything. I observe everything, and share my observations of the world with others. And then, most importantly, I listen to their observations. I share their experiences with them. I accept them as just as valid as my own.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p graf--trailing" id="275a" name="275a">Once I shifted my perspective, the world looked like a very different place. So this is my intellectual revolution. Sharing my experiences with the world. I have studied so many things for so long. I just never understood why until now.</p></div></div></section>
