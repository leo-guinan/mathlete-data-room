<section class="section section--body section--first section--last" name="0115"><div class="section-divider"><hr class="section-divider"/></div><div class="section-content"><div class="section-inner sectionLayout--insetColumn"><h3 class="graf graf--h3 graf--leading graf--title" id="916c" name="916c">What is Chaos?</h3><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--h3" id="1454" name="1454">How I Understand it, and how I Use it in my Life</p><figure class="graf graf--figure graf-after--p" id="140a" name="140a"><img alt="abstract artwork that appears chaotic in nature" class="graf-image" data-height="4971" data-image-id="0*lRTdmHKhgZ_rEEZb" data-is-featured="true" data-unsplash-photo-id="Z7n-qSootxg" data-width="3459" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*lRTdmHKhgZ_rEEZb"/><figcaption class="imageCaption">Photo by <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com/@markusspiske?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com/@markusspiske?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-creator noopener" target="_blank">Markus Spiske</a> on <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-source noopener" target="_blank">Unsplash</a></figcaption></figure><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--figure" id="14bc" name="14bc">So what is chaos? I googled it, and got this back: “complete disorder and confusion.”</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="a4fc" name="a4fc">I don’t completely agree with that. So I am going to define Chaos to be: “apparent complete disorder and confusion for which the direct causes cannot be known at this time.”</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="6b3e" name="6b3e">I think that drastically alters the ways we can view Chaos. Even if we don’t completely understand how the Chaos came to be, what happens if we can accept that there are things we cannot currently fully explain?</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="819b" name="819b">I have always felt a bit Chaotic. How so? I would find myself doing things “just because”. So I started to learn about myself. I tried figuring myself out completely. I researched my medical diagnoses. I read and read and read. I wanted to overcome myself. And I did. Now I feel Chaotic, but I know how to direct the Chaos.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="c945" name="c945">I want to revisit my definition of Chaos: “apparent complete disorder and confusion for which the direct causes cannot be known <strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">at this time</strong>.”</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="ca13" name="ca13">That part in bold is the key. Sometimes we need to understand that there are just things that don’t make complete sense right now. And that is fine. We cannot know everything.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p graf--trailing" id="cf2f" name="cf2f">Now I understand my Chaos. And I know how to direct my Chaos. I invite everyone to try to understand their own Chaos. You may find out some very interesting things about yourself. I know I did.</p></div></div></section>
