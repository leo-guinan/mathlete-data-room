<section class="e-content" data-field="body">
<section class="section section--body section--first section--last" name="0d67"><div class="section-divider"><hr class="section-divider"/></div><div class="section-content"><div class="section-inner sectionLayout--insetColumn"><h3 class="graf graf--h3 graf--leading graf--title" id="efd1" name="efd1">My 3 simple rules for designing systems</h3><h4 class="graf graf--h4 graf-after--h3 graf--subtitle" id="e944" name="e944">Both Human and Computer Systems.</h4></div><div class="section-inner sectionLayout--outsetColumn"><figure class="graf graf--figure graf--layoutOutsetCenter graf-after--h4" id="fcb9" name="fcb9"><img alt="Starfield" class="graf-image" data-height="4000" data-image-id="0*p6Q7UY1khvEC6qo6" data-is-featured="true" data-unsplash-photo-id="bTmhh-Exx9s" data-width="6000" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1200/0*p6Q7UY1khvEC6qo6"/><figcaption class="imageCaption">Photo by <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com/@weirick?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com/@weirick?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-creator noopener noopener" target="_blank">Jake Weirick</a> on <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-source noopener noopener" target="_blank">Unsplash</a></figcaption></figure></div><div class="section-inner sectionLayout--insetColumn"><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--figure" id="7aa5" name="7aa5">I have been thinking a lot about systems lately. Specifically, the intersections of human systems and computer systems. I came up with three pretty simple rules for successful systems.</p><ul class="postList"><li class="graf graf--li graf-after--p" id="d838" name="d838">Speak of I</li><li class="graf graf--li graf-after--li" id="2677" name="2677">Think of We</li><li class="graf graf--li graf-after--li" id="426e" name="426e">Forget You</li></ul><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--li" id="545d" name="545d">These don’t sound like they can relate to computer systems. But let’s examine each in more detail.</p><h3 class="graf graf--h3 graf-after--p" id="3424" name="3424"><strong class="markup--strong markup--h3-strong">1. Speak of I</strong></h3><figure class="graf graf--figure graf-after--h3" id="3746" name="3746"><img alt="Man with beard an hat in front of neon sign" class="graf-image" data-height="7952" data-image-id="0*37OG2rkhWapKTI8J" data-unsplash-photo-id="db2z4cwK-Lw" data-width="5304" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*37OG2rkhWapKTI8J"/><figcaption class="imageCaption">Photo by <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com/@rawdyl?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com/@rawdyl?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-creator noopener" target="_blank">Dylan Sauerwein</a> on <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-source noopener" target="_blank">Unsplash</a></figcaption></figure><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--figure" id="6605" name="6605">The only knowledge you have for certain is your experience. In order to share your experience, you need to openly share the observations of the environment as you can see it. For a computer system, have the local system collect its own data, and report its observations up the chain.</p><h3 class="graf graf--h3 graf-after--p" id="29c6" name="29c6"><strong class="markup--strong markup--h3-strong">2. Think of We</strong></h3><figure class="graf graf--figure graf-after--h3" id="c37f" name="c37f"><img alt="Group of people protesting" class="graf-image" data-height="4000" data-image-id="0*SGmEQ1hYdP7JWo6a" data-unsplash-photo-id="72doRdFx-Lo" data-width="6000" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*SGmEQ1hYdP7JWo6a"/><figcaption class="imageCaption">Photo by <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com/@theeastlondonphotographer?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com/@theeastlondonphotographer?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-creator noopener" target="_blank">Ehimetalor Akhere Unuabona</a> on <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-source noopener" target="_blank">Unsplash</a></figcaption></figure><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--figure" id="da3b" name="da3b">When trying to decide on a future state to pursue, it is important to think of yourself in the context of the overall system. What are the goals of the system? How can a local system easily report its observations up the chain? Is there a system to take data from lower level systems and compile it?</p><h3 class="graf graf--h3 graf-after--p" id="d619" name="d619"><strong class="markup--strong markup--h3-strong">3. Forget You</strong></h3><figure class="graf graf--figure graf-after--h3" id="6a78" name="6a78"><img alt="Man pointing at viewer" class="graf-image" data-height="3456" data-image-id="0*XHAw2QwYXvGM4nR1" data-unsplash-photo-id="-KobSuU7b3g" data-width="5184" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*XHAw2QwYXvGM4nR1"/><figcaption class="imageCaption">Photo by <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com/@adigold1?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com/@adigold1?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-creator noopener" target="_blank">Adi Goldstein</a> on <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-source noopener" target="_blank">Unsplash</a></figcaption></figure><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--figure" id="01ee" name="01ee">The idea of “You” can be a big problem, both in human systems and computer systems. Why is this? It is really only a problem when one system is forcing its observation of its environment onto a different system.
