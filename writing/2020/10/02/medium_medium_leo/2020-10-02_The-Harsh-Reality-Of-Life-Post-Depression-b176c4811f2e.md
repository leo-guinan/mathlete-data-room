<section class="section section--body section--first section--last" name="3522"><div class="section-divider"><hr class="section-divider"/></div><div class="section-content"><div class="section-inner sectionLayout--insetColumn"><h3 class="graf graf--h3 graf--leading graf--title" id="3e00" name="3e00">The Harsh Reality Of Life Post Depression</h3><h4 class="graf graf--h4 graf-after--h3 graf--subtitle" id="fd59" name="fd59">I am Finally Coming Out of my Shell to a Different World then it was when I Went In.</h4><figure class="graf graf--figure graf-after--h4" id="db55" name="db55"><img class="graf-image" data-height="3456" data-image-id="0*mXYhW-JANissamxi" data-is-featured="true" data-unsplash-photo-id="6x2iKGi6SPU" data-width="5184" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*mXYhW-JANissamxi"/><figcaption class="imageCaption">Photo by <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com/@cferdo?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com/@cferdo?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-creator noopener" target="_blank">Fernando @cferdo</a> on <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-source noopener" target="_blank">Unsplash</a></figcaption></figure><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--figure" id="77ea" name="77ea">I realized something today. I realized my real-life social network is not quite what I thought it was. I have written before about my struggles with mental health. For a long time, it ate at me. The more I write, the more I understand myself though. One thing I have started to realize is how important a strong social network is.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="a1d3" name="a1d3">I was pretty much stuck in my own head for about nine years. The problem with that is it doesn’t let you develop strong social networks. I have been trying to draw on some older social networks that I had developed, but relationships atrophy with age when not given the care they deserve.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="f1b7" name="f1b7">I am now starting to come out of my shell, but I am realizing more and more how little the people around me actually know me. They think they do, but when I act like myself they are all confused. I believe I have a Big Idea though, and it is fueling me. My family and friends don’t understand.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p graf--trailing" id="6861" name="6861">I am not sure where my social networks go from here. I have started reaching out to different social networks online to try to connect with other people in different ways. I am also facing some difficult choices right now. I think it might be time to make some changes. My network is not what I need it to be right now. I just need to figure out exactly what I need.</p></div></div></section>
