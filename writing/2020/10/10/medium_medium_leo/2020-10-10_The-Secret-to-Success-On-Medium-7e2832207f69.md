I also spend quite a bit writing anymore. And occasionally working. As I was flipping through story after story, I noticed a very important pattern in the most successful stories.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="8722" name="8722">They were stories of lives that are being lived. They are shared experiences. They are knowledge gained.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="2dae" name="2dae">That is the way that people become successful on Medium. Or really, any platform like Medium.</p><h4 class="graf graf--h4 graf-after--p" id="de62" name="de62">Defining Success</h4><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--h4" id="f816" name="f816">It doesn’t take a lot to be successful. I am extremely successful as a professional writer after having been writing for 1.5 months. I made $38 my first month of writing. Already up above $10 this month. I have written a ton over the past month and a half or so. It helps me organize my ideas, get them out into the world, connect with people, and reflect on experiences in my life and the effect they had on me. And each one of these stories can continue to earn money now.</p><h4 class="graf graf--h4 graf-after--p" id="f97d" name="f97d">The Value of Content</h4><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--h4" id="79be" name="79be">This is the true value in content. When selling your time for money, you get money for actions performed at a single time. When monetizing your content, you write it once and then just let it collect money. Each story is a penny in the bank collecting compound interest. And at a much higher rate than any bank, although you may not see it yet.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="0a6b" name="0a6b">I have seen failure. My biggest failure was also my biggest success. Irony is a very strong force in my life.</p><h4 class="graf graf--h4 graf-after--p" id="fe24" name="fe24">The Failure</h4><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--h4" id="5105" name="5105">I was watching how people made money on Medium. I figured out how I could too. All it took was a carefully crafted attack. I could take my emotions and use them to create reactions in other people.
