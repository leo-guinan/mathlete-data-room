<section class="section section--body section--first section--last" name="27fb"><div class="section-divider"><hr class="section-divider"/></div><div class="section-content"><div class="section-inner sectionLayout--insetColumn"><h3 class="graf graf--h3 graf--leading graf--title" id="2897" name="2897">My Plan: Give the World F*ck You Money</h3><h4 class="graf graf--h4 graf-after--h3 graf--subtitle" id="df1f" name="df1f">Everyone Needs That Freedom</h4><figure class="graf graf--figure graf-after--h4" id="1564" name="1564"><img class="graf-image" data-height="3648" data-image-id="0*D0o5oOiR-zePz4h7" data-is-featured="true" data-unsplash-photo-id="FI4NKaiTePY" data-width="5472" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*D0o5oOiR-zePz4h7"/><figcaption class="imageCaption">Photo by <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com/@synkevych?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com/@synkevych?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-creator noopener" target="_blank">Roman Synkevych</a> on <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-source noopener" target="_blank">Unsplash</a></figcaption></figure><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--figure" id="d51c" name="d51c">I have been thinking a lot about social issues lately. I wanted to understand what was actually happening in the world. That is how I ended up creating a new economic model that I am using to launch my startup.</p><blockquote class="graf graf--pullquote graf-after--p" id="5344" name="5344">I want to give you freedom. That is my business plan.</blockquote><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--pullquote" id="dbdf" name="dbdf">Money makes everything hard. Have you ever found yourself sticking in a relationship, or a job, or an otherwise bad situation because you didn’t think you could afford to leave?</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="5ada" name="5ada">That makes no damn sense. Why should money prevent us being happy?</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="ad99" name="ad99">Money can’t buy happiness. Lack of money is extremely unhealthy. So it can buy health.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="370a" name="370a">It is a lot easier to be happy when you are healthy.</p><blockquote class="graf graf--pullquote graf-after--p" id="6e1f" name="6e1f">Money may not buy happiness, but it certainly helps.</blockquote><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--pullquote" id="c0fa" name="c0fa">My goal is to give you an opportunity to earn enough money that you don’t have to stay in bad situations. If you need to leave, you can.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="5587" name="5587">That’s called Fuck You Money.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="e1ae" name="e1ae">I want the world to have the chance to leave shitty jobs. To leave shitty relationships.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="cfa2" name="cfa2">I recently listened to an interview with venture capitalist <a class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" data-href="http://www.bedrockcap.com/geoff-lewis" href="http://www.bedrockcap.com/geoff-lewis" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Geoff Lewis</a>. Somebody asked him about Fuck You Money. He said that he didn’t need it. He had a quote that I want to share.</p><blockquote class="graf graf--blockquote graf-after--p" id="d2df" name="d2df">It’s not Fuck You Money. I don’t want to fuck with anyone.</blockquote><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--blockquote" id="5e45" name="5e45">I started thinking about that. He doesn’t need Fuck You Money because he is wealthy and has been able to craft his life to his wants and needs.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="46ad" name="46ad"><a class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" data-href="https://medium.com/illumination-curated/the-rise-of-fake-news-be8c2e32c0e" href="https://medium.com/illumination-curated/the-rise-of-fake-news-be8c2e32c0e" target="_blank">As part of my economic model, I realized we live in two different economies. </a>We live in the Have Economy, which grows exponentially, and we live in the Have Not Economy, which grows linearly.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="1c21" name="1c21">If you are a part of the Have Economy, you don’t need Fuck You Money. You already have it and it is growing exponentially.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="b4cd" name="b4cd">The people that need Fuck You Money are in the linear Have Not Economy. They are the ones stuck in unhealthy situations because they can’t afford to leave them.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="7746" name="7746">If we want a healthy society and a healthy world, everyone needs to have Fuck You Money. Then people get to vote with their wallets.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="45f3" name="45f3">Going to be an asshole? Bye.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="7794" name="7794">Cheap shitty food? Bye.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="1f29" name="1f29">Unsafe older cars that are nothing but money pits holding you back? Bye.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="f466" name="f466">If everyone has the freedom of choice, the choices get better for everyone. Imagine what our world would be like if everyone could afford to craft it according to their desires. Imagine how much easier it would be to introduce revolutionary technology. If everyone can afford it, you can upgrade everything instead of having to support older technology that is no longer optimal.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="4a8a" name="4a8a">We stop wasting resources on things that don’t provide the same level of value. This also gives us the ability to fix the planet. If everyone can get electric cars, no one needs to use gas anymore. That is the level that I want us to be at.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="0879" name="0879">That is my plan. I have a ton of ideas on how we can get there. We will get there. It is just a matter of time.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="c7fa" name="c7fa"><a class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" data-href="https://research.guinantech.com/newsletter" href="https://research.guinantech.com/newsletter" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Interested in following my progress?
