<section class="section section--body section--first section--last" name="b84b"><div class="section-divider"><hr class="section-divider"/></div><div class="section-content"><div class="section-inner sectionLayout--insetColumn"><h3 class="graf graf--h3 graf--leading graf--title" id="e232" name="e232">Move Over, Internet of Things. It’s Time For the Internet of You.</h3><h4 class="graf graf--h4 graf-after--h3 graf--subtitle" id="b1cf" name="b1cf">The Next Shift in the Internet’s Evolution is Here. What Does That Mean for You?</h4><figure class="graf graf--figure graf-after--h4" id="235c" name="235c"><img class="graf-image" data-height="3648" data-image-id="0*j3X5mppB8tZHetLa" data-is-featured="true" data-unsplash-photo-id="LGNV-4-l8LA" data-width="5472" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*j3X5mppB8tZHetLa"/><figcaption class="imageCaption">Photo by <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com/@olegmagni?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com/@olegmagni?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-creator noopener" target="_blank">Oleg Magni</a> on <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-source noopener" target="_blank">Unsplash</a></figcaption></figure><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--figure" id="8fc4" name="8fc4">I have been studying how the rise of the internet contributed to today’s society. <a class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" data-href="https://medium.com/illumination-curated/the-rise-of-fake-news-be8c2e32c0e" href="https://medium.com/illumination-curated/the-rise-of-fake-news-be8c2e32c0e" target="_blank">I explored the rise of “fake news”</a>. Fake news is the result of computers influencing our society. <a class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" data-href="https://medium.com/the-innovation/humanity-failed-the-turing-test-69c4f79a169d" href="https://medium.com/the-innovation/humanity-failed-the-turing-test-69c4f79a169d" target="_blank">Computers introduced a break in the human economy and gave those with the knowledge the ability to earn as much money as they want.</a></p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="f118" name="f118">I did the math and designed the system. <a class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" data-href="https://medium.com/illumination-curated/i-figured-out-how-to-implement-ubi-from-the-private-sector-f0999b0a6a04" href="https://medium.com/illumination-curated/i-figured-out-how-to-implement-ubi-from-the-private-sector-f0999b0a6a04" target="_blank">There is a way to create universal basic income for everyone.</a> That way, everyone benefits. The pandemic has shown us that life is unpredictable. <a class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" data-href="https://newsroom.unsw.edu.au/news/business-law/3-useful-things-know-about-fear-economics-and-how-contain-it" href="https://newsroom.unsw.edu.au/news/business-law/3-useful-things-know-about-fear-economics-and-how-contain-it" rel="noopener" target="_blank">The one thing that makes unpredictability scary? Money.</a></p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="ad5a" name="ad5a">I want to stop the cycles of fear that I have been seeing. People are by nature good. They try to abide by society’s rules. But when society’s rules don’t make sense, people get creative. They figure out ways to bend and break the rules in order to ensure their survival and the survival of people close to them. Computers helped us figure out how to make that profitable. So fear became the way to influence behavior. It was the most effective way to get people’s attention. Play to their fears. That is how we ended up with Donald Trump as the president.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="e5dd" name="e5dd">The problem is that that breaks people’s brains. It causes them to see one reality through the lens of social media and another outside of it. Depending on where on social media you tend to stay, your view of reality is limited. Computers ensured that. It is easy to make money quickly by playing to people’s fears. It is much easier to sell you products if you are afraid. <a class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" data-href="https://www.stress.org/please-meet-your-monkey-mind" href="https://www.stress.org/please-meet-your-monkey-mind" rel="noopener" target="_blank">You start to revert to your monkey brain and you can be controlled.</a></p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="6770" name="6770">Once advertisers start controlling your behavior, you have to start working harder and harder to break free from their influence. Otherwise, you get caught in their nets of influence. Then you stop doing the things you want to do and start doing what you are being convinced to do. That’s where a lot of mental illness comes in.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="e1ba" name="e1ba">To be clear, I am not blaming anyone for this. It wasn’t done intentionally. There were a million factors in play that people didn’t understand at the time. But it is time for a change now that we realize what is happening.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="e145" name="e145">The change begins with and centers around you. The future of the internet is whatever you make it for yourself. The internet is a personalized playground for you. It will give you access to the best information immediately and give you the opportunity to evolve as you decide you need to. Figure out how to harness that power and use it to change your life. Once you figure out how to change your life?</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="54a1" name="54a1">Share it with the world. If it can change your life, maybe it can change someone else’s.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="28e9" name="28e9">That is the future. Customize it for you. Share it with others.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="0318" name="0318">Technology and humanity have a bright future together. We just need clear boundaries.</p><h3 class="graf graf--h3 graf-after--p" id="afd7" name="afd7">References</h3><h4 class="graf graf--h4 graf-after--h3" id="cf51" name="cf51">Previous Stories</h4><div
