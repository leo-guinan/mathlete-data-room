Refining What I am Trying to Build
</section>
<section class="e-content" data-field="body">
<section class="section section--body section--first section--last" name="63e8"><div class="section-divider"><hr class="section-divider"/></div><div class="section-content"><div class="section-inner sectionLayout--insetColumn"><h3 class="graf graf--h3 graf--leading graf--title" id="4f54" name="4f54">Reflecting on My Startup Journey: Week 2</h3><h4 class="graf graf--h4 graf-after--h3 graf--subtitle" id="1277" name="1277">Refining What I am Trying to Build</h4><figure class="graf graf--figure graf-after--h4" id="4b8c" name="4b8c"><img class="graf-image" data-height="3699" data-image-id="0*IsCItgHdBqarc9hh" data-is-featured="true" data-unsplash-photo-id="kn-UmDZQDjM" data-width="5548" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*IsCItgHdBqarc9hh"/><figcaption class="imageCaption">Photo by <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com/@xavi_cabrera?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com/@xavi_cabrera?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-creator noopener" target="_blank">Xavi Cabrera</a> on <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-source noopener" target="_blank">Unsplash</a></figcaption></figure><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--figure" id="5eae" name="5eae">I have swirled a bit on what exactly I want to build. I started going back and forth a bit.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="ddf5" name="ddf5">But now I have clarity. I needed to step back a bit. I kept trying to mutate my ideas to match others’ goals. In the end, I had to rediscover my dream and make sure that I am on target for that. I can help others reach their dreams, but I don’t need to give mine up in order to do so. It just required more careful thought.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="90d5" name="90d5">That has been the hardest part for me. I really want to keep rushing. That is what startup culture keeps telling us is the key. It is all about the speed, right?</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="b8be" name="b8be">Sometimes. But sometimes, it is better to wait. Wisdom is achieved when you know how to tell the difference.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="c4fe" name="c4fe">There is a way to practice though. It is what I have been doing. I work diligently for a week. Then I reflect on what I have accomplished.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="a63e" name="a63e">If it isn’t on target, I ruthlessly readjust my course. I very much have shiny-object syndrome. I have a broad vision for what I want to create. That makes it very easy to go off into the weeds. I need to maintain my focus at the correct resolution. That has been the hardest part for me so far. I still have the engineering mindset. When I see a problem, I start fixing it. I am learning how to prioritize problems better.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="ccf5" name="ccf5">It is still a lonely journey. When you are the only one who really sees your vision of the future, you spend all your time trying to help others see it. When it is pretty far away from what we have now? It takes a lot of effort to make it happen. People are not easily convinced. I am learning this</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="84db" name="84db">But eventually, I will be able to show it to someone and they will truly see it. And that will make it all worth it. Then, growth will truly be possible.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="f2f6" name="f2f6"><a class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" data-href="https://leoguinan.substack.com/" href="https://leoguinan.substack.com/" rel="noopener" target="_blank"><em class="markup--em markup--p-em">If you want to follow my journey from corporate software engineer to startup founder, sign up for my mailing list!</em></a></p><h4 class="graf graf--h4 graf-after--p" id="66d9" name="66d9">Previous Weeks</h4><div class="graf graf--mixtapeEmbed graf-after--h4 graf--trailing" id="f09d" name="f09d"><a class="markup--anchor markup--mixtapeEmbed-anchor" data-href="https://medium.com/illumination-curated/reflecting-on-my-startup-journey-week-one-3b27ec25f0ec" href="https://medium.com/illumination-curated/reflecting-on-my-startup-journey-week-one-3b27ec25f0ec" title="https://medium.com/illumination-curated/reflecting-on-my-startup-journey-week-one-3b27ec25f0ec"><strong class="markup--strong markup--mixtapeEmbed-strong">Reflecting on My Startup Journey: Week One</strong><br/><em class="markup--em markup--mixtapeEmbed-em">What the Hell am I Doing?</em>medium.com</a><a class="js-mixtapeImage mixtapeImage u-ignoreBlock" data-media-id="41958923d7fc9415cbfcd30fb5c93e63" data-thumbnail-img-id="0*UuAhS-EsWnfKCxy4" href="https://medium.com/illumination-curated/reflecting-on-my-startup-journey-week-one-3b27ec25f0ec" style="background-image: url(https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/fit/c/160/160/0*UuAhS-EsWnfKCxy4);"></a></div></div></div></section>
