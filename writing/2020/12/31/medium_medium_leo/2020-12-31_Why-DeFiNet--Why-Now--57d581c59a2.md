But never anything huge.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="f6ce" name="f6ce">I learned some more. I read Medium articles on how to make more money on Medium. (A lot of them, if I am honest.)</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="bd3a" name="bd3a">At some point, I realized something. The top writers don’t make money for writing on Medium. Let’s start one of those too! And let’s form an LLC!</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="c3a9" name="c3a9">I did it all, hoping to start making some extra money. But instead, I decided to pivot.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="d69d" name="d69d">I have spent the last decade as a software engineer. If you put me through a painful process, I like to build something to make it less painful.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="e809" name="e809">DeFiNet is the something I am building. I saw where the pain points were in the process of trying to make an income online and I see where I can build something better. I have a few ideas in mind, but in the end, it comes down to the users that I am building for.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="a039" name="a039">To that end, I invite you to join our community. Before I build a product, I want to grow a community. I want to make sure that I build the right thing. The community will let me know if I am building it right.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p graf--trailing" id="4ac0" name="4ac0">Be a part of the community that helps you make money.
