That’s something that writing has helped me process. That’s a theme in my writing, but I try to keep the focus elsewhere when I write on Medium.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="a345" name="a345">If you are interested in how Autism/ADHD affects my life and startup, <a class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" data-href="https://adhdstartup.substack.com/" href="https://adhdstartup.substack.com/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">check out my Substack</a>.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="3f93" name="3f93">On Medium, my writing focuses a lot on what I am learning as I launch my startup. In doing so, I realized that I had the opportunity to follow my dreams.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="28fd" name="28fd">Writing helped me realize what those dreams were. That’s when I started to discover the true potential of the creator economy. It was way bigger than I had realized. But I also saw an economy that has it drastically undervalued.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="1d1e" name="1d1e">That’s about the time I met my cofounder. We met on Y Combinator Startup School’s forum. It ended up way more time-consuming than I was hoping, but, while I didn’t secure funding, I did learn a lot about the business side of startups. And now it’s time to bootstrap.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="17c3" name="17c3">In addition to the startup, I am going all-in as a content creator as well.
