<section class="section section--body section--first section--last" name="0782"><div class="section-divider"><hr class="section-divider"/></div><div class="section-content"><div class="section-inner sectionLayout--insetColumn"><h4 class="graf graf--h4 graf--leading graf--kicker" id="148a" name="148a">DeFiNet NFT</h4><h3 class="graf graf--h3 graf-after--h4 graf--title" id="c70b" name="c70b">NFTs For Medium</h3><h4 class="graf graf--h4 graf-after--h3 graf--subtitle" id="56da" name="56da">Let’s Model It Like The Soccer Transfer Market</h4><figure class="graf graf--figure graf-after--h4" id="5e3c" name="5e3c"><img class="graf-image" data-height="4000" data-image-id="0*EyrDDFy-H4W9oDpy" data-is-featured="true" data-unsplash-photo-id="JO19K0HDDXI" data-width="6000" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*EyrDDFy-H4W9oDpy"/><figcaption class="imageCaption">Photo by <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com/@travelnomades?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com/@travelnomades?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-creator noopener" target="_blank">Travel Nomades</a> on <a class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" data-href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" rel="photo-source noopener" target="_blank">Unsplash</a></figcaption></figure><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--figure" id="6876" name="6876">Like many writers, I have been watching the Non-Fungible Token space with interest. It seems like a good way to make some quick money. I’ve been exploring several options. But I saw problems with all of them.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="bdee" name="bdee">That’s when I analyzed what NFTs were and why they are becoming popular. The goal is partial ownership of digital property. When that property is sold, all owners receive a portion of the proceeds. Depending on the offering, buyers may receive full or partial ownership. You can do a bunch of cool things when talking about percentage ownership.</p><h4 class="graf graf--h4 graf-after--p" id="83b6" name="83b6">NFTs and Soccer</h4><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--h4" id="268b" name="268b">I was looking for a metaphor to explain NFTs when I realized there is already a real-world equivalent. Soccer teams all over the world create NFTs with their talent. It creates fractional ownership of a player’s rights from their academy team to their pro team(s). I’m a big soccer fan and one of the most interesting parts of the game to me is the transfer market.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="8dbe" name="8dbe">Also, I used to play a lot of <a class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" data-href="https://www.footballmanager.com/" href="https://www.footballmanager.com/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Football Manager</a>. So if you want to think about NFTs and you are a soccer fan, imagine it this way: NFTs allow people to buy sell-on clauses to digital property. When that property gets sold in the future, you get a percentage of it, depending on how the NFT is structured. You can also own 100% of it, but that use case is boring.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="c2df" name="c2df">As a creator, I am like the academy getting the player ready for the pros. When my player is ready, I put it on the transfer market. Right now, we are seeing the market for clubs like Real Madrid, PSG, Juventus, and Manchester City. Those are interesting because of the big numbers.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="f2fd" name="f2fd">But that’s never been terribly interesting. Everybody looks at the top.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="7370" name="7370">What happens in the lower tiers of soccer? I am a fan of Columbus Crew SC, in Major League Soccer in the US. I have spent way too many hours looking at transfer data for players the club is rumored to be interested in.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="9448" name="9448">And that to me is where this becomes relevant to Medium. The idea of fractional ownership got me thinking about how the most successful writers on Medium become successful. They’ve developed relationships along the way that have helped them build out their audience at various stages.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="bd8c" name="bd8c">And none of that value gets captured. The problem with Medium is that it pays the writers only. But there is a need for editorial work. There is a need for distribution. They haven’t figured out where to put that in the stack.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="4a8b" name="4a8b">The answer is in the ownership of the article. What if you could give out ownership in your article in exchange for various services? Suddenly, the environment becomes much more collaborative in nature.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="ccb6" name="ccb6">If you could easily collaborate and not have to worry about who gets what part? It becomes frictionless to collaborate. Imagine the possibilities.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="6322" name="6322">This article is the first example of what we can accomplish.</p><h4 class="graf graf--h4 graf-after--p" id="bf22" name="bf22">Ownership:</h4><ul class="postList"><li class="graf graf--li graf-after--h4" id="3616" name="3616">Author: 60%</li><li class="graf graf--li graf-after--li" id="9db6" name="9db6">Reserved: 20% (I have some ideas for this chunk. Marking it out and will figure out the specifics later.)</li><li class="graf graf--li graf-after--li" id="5394" name="5394">Audience: 10%<br/>10 people will each own 1%. See below for details.</li><li class="graf graf--li graf-after--li" id="1ebb" name="1ebb">DeFiNet: 10%</li></ul><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--li" id="bd1c" name="bd1c">As part of this experiment, I am going to give 5 subscribers to my mailing list 1% of this article’s earnings through a random drawing.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="4202" name="4202">Want to guarantee you get a piece? Just get the most referrals. The top 5 subscribers with the most referrals will also get 1%.</p><p class="graf graf--p graf-after--p" id="4371" name="4371">How much is this article worth? You decide. Is it worth a share? That makes it more valuable.
